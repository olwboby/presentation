Characters

Many characters have appeared in the latest 2017 trailer for God of War , although only a few have a name. Kratos is the main character, as he appears to be on an adventure with his son, Atreus. Several other people appeared during the trailer although remained unnamed. Since this is a God of War, there were also voices that seemed connected to various Norse Gods, one including the World Serpent.

  • Kratos
  • Atreus
  • The Witch of Woods (Freya)
  • Mimir
  • Sindri
  • Brok
  • The Stranger (Baldur)
  • World Serpent
  • Loki
  • Odin
  • Thor
